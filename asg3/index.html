Lauren's Virtual World
Move: WASD   Turn: Q E
Mouse look: Left Click to lock   Esc to release
Edit: R = add block, F = delete block
fps=0
How to play (Catch the Rats):
1) Walk around the world and find the rats.
2) Get close to a rat to catch it (it will disappear).
3) Catch all 15 rats to win. You’ll see YOU WIN! on the screen.
